Kazimierz Sliwa


I am currently a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy in Heidelberg, Germany working with Dr. Eva Schinnerer.

My primary research consists of studying the molecular gas content of Ultra/Luminous Infrared Galaxies (U/LIRGs) that reside in the local universe. I use high-resolution interferometric data from ALMA, CARMA, SMA and PdBI (now NOEMA) and a non-LTE method to constrain the temperature, volume and column densities of the molecular gas.
